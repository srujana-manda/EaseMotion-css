Exit Animations

Slide-out, zoom-out, flip-out, and fade-out variants — perfectly paired with existing entrance animations.

Fade-out

ease-fade-out & directional variants

Element fades away, optionally sliding in a direction at the same time. Pairs with ease-fade-in / ease-fade-in-*.

.ease-fade-out
Fade
.ease-fade-out-up
Up
.ease-fade-out-down
Down
.ease-fade-out-left
Left
.ease-fade-out-right
Right
Pairs with: .ease-fade-in, .ease-fade-in-*
Slide-out

ease-slide-out-up / -down / -left / -right

Element slides off-screen in the chosen direction while fading. Pairs with ease-slide-in-*.

.ease-slide-out-up
Up
.ease-slide-out-down
Down
.ease-slide-out-left
Left
.ease-slide-out-right
Right
Pairs with: .ease-slide-in-*
Zoom & Flip

ease-zoom-out & ease-flip-out

Zooms out (scale + fade) or flips out (3D X-axis rotation). Pairs with ease-zoom-in and ease-flip-in.

.ease-zoom-out
Zoom
.ease-flip-out
Flip
Pairs with: .ease-zoom-in, .ease-flip-in
