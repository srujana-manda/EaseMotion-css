FAQ

Common Questions

Everything you need to know before getting started.

How long does it take to become fluent? ▼

Most learners reach conversational fluency in 3–6 months with 15 minutes of daily practice. Advanced fluency typically takes 9–12 months depending on the language and your prior experience.

Can I learn multiple languages at once? ▼

Yes! Premium and Family plans allow unlimited languages. We recommend focusing on one language at a time for best results, but many learners successfully study 2–3 languages simultaneously.

How does the AI pronunciation coach work? ▼

Our AI analyzes your speech at the phoneme level, identifying exactly which sounds need improvement. It provides visual feedback (waveforms and mouth position diagrams) and targeted exercises for problem sounds.

Is Lingva suitable for children? ▼

Absolutely! Our Family plan includes a separate children's mode with gamified lessons, colorful interfaces, and parental controls to manage screen time and track progress.

What happens if I cancel my subscription? ▼

You keep access to Premium features until the end of your billing period. After that, your account reverts to Free tier — you won't lose any progress or saved vocabulary.
