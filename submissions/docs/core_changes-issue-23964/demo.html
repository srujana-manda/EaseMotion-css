ease-overflow-anchor

CSS utility classes for overflow-anchor (scroll anchoring)

Demo: Scroll Anchoring Behavior

Scroll down in each panel, then click "Insert Above" to add content at the top. Watch how auto maintains your scroll position while none lets it jump.

overflow-anchor: auto .ease-overflow-anchor-auto
New content inserted above will appear here
overflow-anchor: none .ease-overflow-anchor-none
New content inserted above will appear here

Tip: Scroll to the bottom of a panel first, then click "Insert Above". The auto panel will keep your viewport stable; the none panel will jump.

Usage

<!-- Enable scroll anchoring (default behavior) --> <div class="ease-overflow-anchor-auto"> Scrollable content... </div> <!-- Disable scroll anchoring --> <div class="ease-overflow-anchor-none"> Scrollable content without anchor adjustment... </div>
