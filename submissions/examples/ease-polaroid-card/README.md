# 📸 Polaroid Card – Tilts and Drops Shadow Like Photo

> A nostalgic, interactive card that tilts and casts a realistic shadow on hover, mimicking the look and feel of a classic polaroid photo.

---

## 📖 Description

The **Polaroid Card** is a nostalgic UI component that replicates the charm of a classic polaroid photo. On hover, the card tilts with a subtle 3D rotation and casts a realistic, multi-layered shadow, while the image inside scales up slightly and a caption fades in. The component features a warm, vintage aesthetic with a cream-colored frame, serif typography, and a clean, photo-like presentation.

The card includes a square image with a polaroid-style white border, a caption overlay that appears on hover, and a footer with location and date information. The tilt effect is enhanced with an optional 3D mouse-follow interaction, making the card feel tactile and responsive. Perfect for photography portfolios, memory galleries, and any application where you want to evoke a sense of nostalgia and warmth.
