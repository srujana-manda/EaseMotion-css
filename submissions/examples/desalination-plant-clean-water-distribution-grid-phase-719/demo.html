Treatment sequence

Five stages, intake to tap

Every liter moves through the same sequence before it reaches a distribution zone. Cards below stagger in on load using the fade-flow-up utility.

STAGE 01

Seawater intake

Screened intake draws from an offshore well, filtering debris and marine life before pumping.

STAGE 02

Pre-filtration

Multi-media and cartridge filters strip sediment and organics ahead of the membranes.

STAGE 03

Reverse osmosis

High-pressure membranes reject dissolved salts, dropping salinity from ~35,000 ppm to under 200.

STAGE 04

Remineralization

Calcium and pH balancing restore mineral content and protect downstream pipework.

STAGE 05

Distribution

Treated water is staged into tanks and released across twelve pressure-managed zones.
