The AetherVoyage Difference

Why Fly With Us

Every detail of the AetherVoyage experience has been engineered for safety, wonder, and unforgettable memory-making.

🛡️

Zero Incident Record

18 consecutive missions. 247 passengers. Zero safety incidents. Our redundant abort systems, autonomous flight AI, and 6-month vehicle inspection cycle make this the world's safest sub-orbital programme.

🎓

Astronaut-Led Training

Your pre-flight program is co-designed by ISS veterans. Learn emergency protocols, practice zero-G manoeuvres in our neutral buoyancy facility, and build the confidence of a real astronaut.

🌍

Panoramic Viewports

Our spacecraft features the industry's largest viewports — 76cm diameter fused silica glass, optically engineered to eliminate distortion so every photograph captures a frame-worthy view of Earth.

📸

In-Flight Media Suite

4K cameras mounted at each viewport and on the exterior of the vehicle capture every moment automatically. Your full flight film is delivered in 8K within 48 hours of landing.

🏥

Flight Medical Support

A board-certified flight surgeon is on-site for every launch and recovery. Comprehensive medical screening, personalised flight physiology advice, and post-landing care are included in every class.

🌱

Carbon Neutral Flights

We offset 200% of every flight's CO₂ through verified reforestation and direct air capture projects. Our next-generation methane-LOX propellant further reduces atmospheric impact by 78% vs. kerosene.
