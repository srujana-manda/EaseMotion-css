High Contrast by Default

Colors are carefully selected to meet or exceed WCAG 2.1 AAA contrast requirements. Text is legible against backgrounds for all users, including those with low vision.

Clear Focus Management

Keyboard navigation is fully supported natively. Active elements receive a prominent 3px solid focus ring with an offset, ensuring users always know their place on the page.

Read focus guidelines

Reduced Motion Respect

The zoom-in animation is disabled for users who prefer reduced motion. We rely on a simple crossfade to prevent triggering vestibular disorders.
