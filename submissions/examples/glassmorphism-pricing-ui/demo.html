Frequently Asked Questions

Can I cancel anytime? +

Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time from your account settings. You will retain access until the end of your current billing cycle.

Do you offer refunds? +

We offer a 14-day money-back guarantee for all new subscriptions. If you're not satisfied, simply contact support within your first two weeks.

Is there a free plan? +

We offer a completely free Starter plan for hobbyists and students, requiring no credit card to sign up forever.

Can I upgrade later? +

Yes, you can upgrade your plan at any time. The cost difference will be prorated based on the time remaining in your current billing cycle.

Do you have team plans? +

Our Business and Enterprise tiers are designed specifically for teams, offering granular role-based access control and shared workspaces.

Is support included? +

All paid plans include priority email support. Business and Enterprise plans include 24/7 live chat and dedicated account managers.

How secure is my data? +

We use enterprise-grade end-to-end encryption. Our servers are SOC2 compliant and undergo regular third-party security audits.

Can I switch billing cycles? +

Absolutely. You can switch between monthly and yearly billing at any time to take advantage of the 20% annual discount.

Are there discounts for nonprofits? +

Yes! We offer a 50% discount on all plans for verified students, educators, and 501(c)(3) registered non-profit organizations.

Do you offer API access? +

API access is included in the Pro, Business, and Enterprise plans. Comprehensive documentation is available in our developer portal.
