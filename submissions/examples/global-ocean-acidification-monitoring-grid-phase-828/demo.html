Research

Scientific Focus Areas

OceanGrid data underpins the world's leading research programmes across marine chemistry, ecology, and climate science.

🧪
Ocean Chemistry

Real-time carbonate system chemistry data — pH, pCO₂, alkalinity, and dissolved inorganic carbon — from sensors calibrated to SOCAT and GLODAP standards.

ISO 17025 Calibrated
🪸
Coral Reef Health

Sub-reef pH monitoring and thermal stress indices feed into NOAA's Coral Reef Watch — providing early-warning bleaching alerts to marine protected areas worldwide.

800+ Reef Sites
🐠
Marine Biodiversity

Environmental DNA capture and acoustic monitoring at sensor buoys delivers species-level biodiversity indices, tracking population trends for 2,400+ marine species.

2,400+ Species Tracked
♻️
Carbon Cycle

Air–sea CO₂ flux measurements from pCO₂ sensors across all basins feed global carbon budget models — quantifying the ocean's role as Earth's primary carbon sink.

2.4 GtC / year
🌐
Climate Modeling

High-frequency ocean state observations assimilated into CMIP7 climate models improve decadal projections of sea-level rise, storm intensity, and acidification trajectories.

CMIP7 Contributor
🛰️
Satellite Observation

Fusion of Copernicus, NASA PACE, and GOES ocean colour satellite imagery with in-situ buoy data creates the highest-resolution global ocean surface picture ever achieved.

1 km² Resolution
